## What is ThriveMap?

ThriveMap is a **behavioral insight system** designed to help you understand how you actually show up over time.

Instead of collecting scattered entries, ThriveMap translates your reflection into clear, grounded insight using **repeating 70-day arcs**.

This is not a journaling app.  
This is a system for **pattern awareness and continuous improvement**.

---

## Core Concept

ThriveMap runs on a simple structure:

- **70-day arcs**
- **10 structured cycles**
- **4 reflection domains**
- Continuous repetition over time

Each arc reveals how you think, act, and return.

Each new arc builds on the last.

---

## What You Get

After each arc, ThriveMap produces:

- **Your strongest signal**  
  The most defining pattern in how you showed up

- **What stands out**  
  A clear, human summary of your behavior

- **Comparison across arcs**  
  How this arc differs from your previous one

- **Carry forward**  
  What to protect or build on next
